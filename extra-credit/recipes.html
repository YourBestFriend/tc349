Baked Potatoes Recipe

A recipe for baked potatoes

Making quality baked potatoes is no easy task, here's how!

Note: Careful not to throw the potatoes away before you bake them!

  • 1.) Preheat Oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  • 2.) Poke holes in potatoes with a fork
  • 3.) Place potatoes in the oven
  • 4.) Close oven
  • 5.) Wait for 50 minutes
  • 6.) Take Potatoes out of oven, Your Done!
