Overview

I actually personally did two of of the five card sorting surveys this time. This
is due to the fact that I had two significantly different ideas of how to organize
the website. Hopefully this isn't bad practice (I suppose it probably is). Also,
this study only contains six cards as the website I want to make for my final project
will not have any huge amount of content on it.

The major difference between the different participants was what was placed on the
main page of the website. There was a fairly even divide between those who wanted
to put "Description of Service" on the home page and those who wanted to put "About
The Company" as the face of the website.
